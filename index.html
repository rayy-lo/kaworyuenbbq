ABOUT

Ka Wor Yuen BBQ was founded in 1998 and we have been cultivating our recipes ever since. Our mission is to share the enticing flavor of Chinese BBQ that is exclusively made here. Sauces used to prepare your meals are made in-house, and are big contributors to the one-of-a-kind taste you get at Ka Wor Yuen BBQ. We strive to serve quality food that will satisfy our customers' heart and soul in a fast and friendly manner. No food leaves the kitchen that we ourselves would not eat.
